MEET THE OFFICERS


President

Yuxuan Chen

Yuxuan Chen is a sophomore at U of M majoring in Computer Science. She started cubing in the summer of 2010 and began going to competitions at the end of 2011. Her favorite events are One-Handed, Skewb, and 3x3x3. Outside of cubing, she enjoys playing random retro video games on her computer, making occasional YouTube videos, and coaching Academic Games.

Average Solve Time: about 13 seconds

Main 3x3x3 Cube: Moyu Hualong

Vice President

Jeremy Kruman

Jeremy Kruman is a junior at the University of Michigan studying computer science. He first got into cubing in 2007, but didn't begin competing until 2013. Aside from cubing, his favorite things to do are play card games and video games, and spend time with friends.

Average Solve Time: about 20.5 seconds

Main 3x3x3 Cube: Gans 356

Secretary

Lauren Klemz

Lauren is a senior (graduating in December 2015!) majoring in Economics and minoring in Applied Statistics. She is a resident advisor for an all freshman hall and she gives free hugs and high fives on the Diag in her spare time. She has been cubing since January 2013 and she loves all of the wonderful people she has met through competitions. At competitions, you can find her entering times into cube comps because data is yummy.

Average Solve Time: 45 seconds

Main 3x3x3 Cube: Moyu Aolong v2
